Lockin App Blocker Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-23

Lockin App Blocker helps you block distracting apps and websites on your device. We do not collect, sell, track, or upload your personal data.

Data Stored On Your Device

Lockin may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Blocked app and website selections.
  • App settings, friction challenge settings, and unlock preferences.
  • Focus or ON-mode history used to display the home screen widget.
  • Purchase status cached after StoreKit confirms a purchase.

iCloud Sync

If you enable Apple iCloud sync, your own app data may sync between devices through your Apple account. We do not operate servers for this sync and cannot access that data.

Network Use

Lockin may connect to third-party market data APIs to load chart data for quiz challenges. These requests do not include your blocked apps, websites, focus history, or personal files. Purchases and restore purchases are handled by Apple's StoreKit services.

Permissions

Lockin uses Apple's Screen Time, Family Controls, Managed Settings, Device Activity, and related system capabilities to let you select and block apps or websites on your device. The selected apps and websites are handled by Apple's system frameworks.

Data Collection

Lockin does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, crash reports, or tracking data. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.
